Phase 3: Traffic Flow Forecasting

        

          
          
          Export forecast CSV
        

      

      
Forecasts are generated for 15, 30, and 60 minutes ahead by approach. The historical-average model is transparent and robust for limited data; gradient boosting is used when enough records exist.

Phase 5: Incident Detection and Human Review

        

          
          
          Export incidents CSV
        

      

      
Phase 5 converts Phase 4 vehicle-candidate detections into explainable incident candidates: congestion events and possible stalled vehicles. All events remain unreviewed until a GAM user marks them as confirmed, false positive, or uncertain.
